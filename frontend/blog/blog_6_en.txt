POST-PANDEMIC RECOVERY SHOWN IN LIFE EXPECTANCY FORECASTS BY THE IFoA
Date: July 3, 2026

The Continuous Mortality Investigation (CMI) committee of the UK's Institute and Faculty of Actuaries (IFoA) has released its latest CMI_2025 mortality projection model.

Key Findings from CMI_2025:
1. Dissipation of Pandemic Anomalies: The heavy impact of anomalous mortality rates observed between 2020 and 2023 has faded. The committee calibrated the new model using stable data from 2024 and 2025.
2. Rising Life Expectancy: A marginal recovery in life expectancy improvements has been recorded in the UK.
3. Impact on Pensions: The new projections suggest pension schemes must increase their liability valuations (reserves) by 0.5% to 1% to cover longer payout durations.
