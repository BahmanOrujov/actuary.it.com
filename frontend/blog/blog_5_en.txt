ACTUARIAL EXAMINATION UPDATE: PYTHON AND MACHINE LEARNING BECOME CORE COMPETENCIES
Date: July 3, 2026

The Society of Actuaries (SOA) and the Casualty Actuarial Society (CAS) have announced significant changes to their education and exam syllabus.

Key Updates:
1. Python Integration: Starting late 2026, preliminary and intermediate exams will require candidates to build models in Python alongside traditional Excel spreadsheets.
2. Predictive Analytics: Forecasting exams will expand deep coverage of machine learning models and stochastic frameworks. Candidates must demonstrate proficiency in Big Data visualization.
3. Transition Phase: A 6-month transition period and free online resources will be provided to help existing candidates adapt to the new topics.

The organizations emphasized that these updates are vital to keeping the actuarial profession aligned with financial technology and data science trends. Next-generation actuaries must master programming and AI tools.
