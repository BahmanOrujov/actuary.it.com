OCTOBER 2026 SUMMIT TO FOCUS ON DIGITAL TRANSFORMATION AND ESG INSURANCE RISKS IN BAKU
Date: July 3, 2026

The International Actuarial Association and the Azerbaijan Insurers Association have released a joint statement announcing that Baku will host the next Annual Global Forum of Actuaries in October 2026.

Forum Agenda:
1. Digital Transformation: The application of AI and Big Data in underwriting and actuarial calculations.
2. Global IFRS 17 Implementation: Lessons learned and operational challenges from various jurisdictions.
3. Green Insurance (ESG): The impact of climate change on actuarial models and the rise of environmental policies.

Over 400 actuaries, regulators, and insurance executives from 50+ countries are expected to attend. The event represents a key step for Azerbaijan's growing insurance market on the global stage.
